Home page

What is a West Marches-style TTRPG? A West Marches-style tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) is a unique gaming format that emphasizes player-driven exploration and adventure in a dynamic, open-world setting. Inspired by the West Marches campaign created by Ben Robbins, this style of gameplay typically involves a large group of players, often divided into smaller parties, exploring a vast and mysterious wilderness. Unlike traditional TTRPGs with fixed storylines and regular sessions, West Marches games are characterized by their episodic nature, allowing players to schedule sessions at their convenience and pursue their own objectives within the shared game world. This site will serve as a platform for creating and maintaining our own West Marches game. Why I'm Passionate About This: I'm passionate about West Marches-style TTRPGs because they represent the epitome of collaborative storytelling and shared imagination. As both a player and a game master, I've experienced firsthand the magic that unfolds when a diverse group of individuals comes together to craft a rich and dynamic game world. The thrill of exploration, the camaraderie forged through adversity, and the sheer joy of discovering the unknown are what drives my enthusiasm for this style of gameplay. Additionally, I'm deeply inspired by the sense of community that flourishes within West Marches campaigns, where players become not just fellow adventurers, but lifelong friends bound by a shared love for storytelling and adventure. Through hosting this project, I hope to create a welcoming space where players can unleash their creativity, forge lasting connections, and embark on epic journeys that will leave a lasting impact on their lives.

Why Join

Why is it Worthwhile? 1. Player Agency and Exploration: In a West Marches campaign, players have unparalleled freedom to explore and shape the game world according to their interests and decisions. The focus on player agency means that every session is filled with exciting discoveries and unexpected challenges, as adventurers chart unexplored territories, uncover ancient ruins, and encounter diverse cultures and creatures. 2. Community Collaboration: One of the greatest strengths of the West Marches format is its ability to foster a strong sense of community among players. Collaborative storytelling and shared experiences create a vibrant gaming environment where players can form alliances, rivalries, and lasting friendships both in and out of character. With a diverse cast of characters and players, each session becomes an opportunity for rich role-playing interactions and memorable moments. 3. Flexibility and Accessibility: Unlike traditional TTRPG campaigns that require a consistent group of players and a fixed schedule, West Marches games offer unparalleled flexibility. With a larger player pool and asynchronous scheduling, participants can easily find sessions that fit their availability, making it ideal for busy schedules and players from different time zones. This accessibility opens up the hobby to a wider audience, welcoming newcomers and seasoned veterans alike to embark on epic adventures together.

What is Needed

What Would Interested GMs/DMs Be Doing? As a Game Master (GM) or Dungeon Master (DM) in a West Marches-style TTRPG, your role is to serve as the arbiter of the game world, creating immersive environments, crafting compelling narratives, and facilitating player-driven exploration and adventure. You'll be responsible for designing the wilderness setting, populating it with diverse locations, challenges, and inhabitants, and responding dynamically to the actions and choices of the players. With each session, you'll collaborate with players to weave their individual stories into the fabric of the larger game world, ensuring that every adventure is unique and memorable. Furthermore, there will be meetings to ensure continuity issues don’t arise and all players feel they are playing in the same living world. What Do Interested Players Need to Do? If you're interested in joining a West Marches-style TTRPG, the first step is to familiarize yourself with the rules and guidelines established by the hosting website or community. Once you've familiarized yourself with the basics, you can create a character that reflects your desired role within the game world, whether it's a cunning rogue, a wise wizard, or a stalwart warrior. From there, you'll have the opportunity to join sessions organized by the GM/DM or even propose your own expeditions and quests for fellow players to join. With a spirit of adventure and a willingness to collaborate with others, you'll embark on an epic journey filled with discovery, danger, and camaraderie in the world of the West Marches.
