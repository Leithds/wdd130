What is a West Marches-style TTRPG? A West Marches-style tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) is a unique gaming format that emphasizes player-driven exploration and adventure in a dynamic, open-world setting. Inspired by the West Marches campaign created by Ben Robbins, this style of gameplay typically involves a large group of players, often divided into smaller parties, exploring a vast and mysterious wilderness. Unlike traditional TTRPGs with fixed storylines and regular sessions, West Marches games are characterized by their episodic nature, allowing players to schedule sessions at their convenience and pursue their own objectives within the shared game world. This site will serve as a platform for creating and maintaining our own West Marches game.

Join our vibrant West Marches-style TTRPG web group and explore boundless realms filled with endless adventure and camaraderie!
